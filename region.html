Winter Wonderland Expedition (5-Day Tour)

Immerse yourself in the diverse wonders of Hokkaido with our 5-day highlights tour. This package combines outdoor adventures and cultural experiences to provide a comprehensive journey through the essence of the region. Traverse the powder-covered slopes of Niseko, known for world-class skiing and snowboarding, and witness the breathtaking cherry blossoms in Furano. Explore the vibrant Sapporo Fish Market, indulge in local delicacies, and enjoy traditional Ainu experiences. From the serenity of Daisetsuzan National Park to the lively summer festivals, this tour captures the heart of Hokkaido in just five unforgettable days.

Autumn Wildlife and Hiking Tour

Experience the untouched beauty of Hokkaido's autumn with our 7-day wildlife and hiking adventure tour. Immerse yourself in the vibrant colors of Biei and traverse the serene landscapes of Kushiro Shitsugen National Park. Marvel at the majestic Red-crowned Cranes in Tsurui and witness the elusive Ezo deer roaming freely in Shikisai-no-Oka. This extended tour allows nature enthusiasts to explore the untamed wilderness of the Shiretoko Peninsula, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where brown bears roam along rugged coastlines. With a perfect blend of wildlife encounters and immersive hiking experiences, this 7-day adventure promises an unforgettable autumn escape in Hokkaido.
